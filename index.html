    Drift Culture in FAMAT

    FAMAT isn't just about math—it's about passion, speed, and community. Our members are as driven as the cars in Initial D, always pushing the limits and taking on new challenges. Whether you're racing to solve a problem or drifting through a tough competition, the thrill is real!

    Teamwork on Every Curve

    Just like a team of racers, FAMAT students work together to achieve greatness. Collaboration, mentorship, and friendly rivalry make every event memorable. Join us and experience the camaraderie!

    Night Practice

    Late-night study sessions are our version of midnight runs. The road to success is lit by dedication and teamwork.

    Precision and Skill

    Every turn, every calculation, every answer—FAMAT students know that precision is key, both on the track and in the classroom.

    Classic Moves

    We honor tradition while blazing new trails. Like the legendary AE86, FAMAT combines history with innovation.

    Bright Ideas, Bright Futures

    Every member brings something unique to the table. FAMAT is a place where bright ideas shine and futures are built—one problem at a time.
